COMPANY OVERVIEW

Galant Shipping & Logistics LLC* is an American based Freight Forwarding Company located in Richmond. GSL* is actively providing services in Air freight, Sea freight, Overland and local Road transportation Services since 2011. GSL* is Partnered Global Network over 7 Continents.

We aim to provide Affordable, fastest and reliable C2C and B2B Shipping and Freight forwarding services to and from Canada. GSL* is a dedicated one stop freight logistics service provider who guarantees professional handling of all Domestic and International Shipments and assures most prolific Shipping reliability. GSL* also offers Inbond cargoes, with timely connections from USA to Canada, and have joined hands with Trucking companies who provide faster and daily runs within Canada and USA at a very low and reasonable cost. Our key feature is same day Express Delivery service within Richmond Locations.

GSL* also offers National and International Courier Services with same day within Richmond and overnight Courier services within USA and to Canada . GSL* is committed to provide Reliable, Cost-effective, Ontime and comprehensive Shipping services.
