Warehousing

  Home ServicesWarehousing

GSL help you with any transportation and storeroom needs for your business. With our 3P contract we have the large bonded warehousing facility equipped with loading docks & pallet jack lifts. We provide competitive storage rates and flexible storage solution.

The warehouse is monitored with its 24 hour security surveillance cameras, fire & theft insurance, planned protection and is kept clean and uncluttered. We also make sure that all stored items are stacked properly in their allocated locations so that nothing gets misplaced or damaged.

Our Services include:

  • Pick & Pack
  • Short/Long term storage
  • Packing
  • Local & International distribution
